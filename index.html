Inspirational thoughts...

E-nitiate

I was once told : « Within a day you go by lots of opportunities. At least one of them is life-changing. »
I believe that E-nitiate is life-changing. It is one of these unique and rare opportunities where you not only get to discover and learn new things, but you also manage to grow up as a person and better yourself. One of the most positive outcomes from this experience is the way the camp affected my expectation on CS. For a long time, I saw in Computer Science something not worth dedicating time to due to its complexity and lack of flexibility. Cinema and stereotypes in general are so misleading. They give you a wrong idea about what CS really is. You would be surprised to know that CS is literally everywhere, from the car you drive to the clothes you wear. There is no limit to CS. And I am glad I've realised this thanks to e-nitiate.
